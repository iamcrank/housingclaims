Complaint Policy

At The House Care, we are dedicated to providing exceptional service to tenants seeking resolution for housing disrepair claims. We recognize that complaints may arise from time to time, and we value your feedback as it helps us enhance our services.

Submitting a Complaint

We encourage tenants to submit their complaints in writing via email or through our online portal.

Include the tenant's name, contact information, a description of the issue, and any relevant documentation.

Acknowledgment and Initial Assessment

Upon receiving a complaint, we will acknowledge receipt within 10 business days.

A designated team member will assess the complaint to understand its nature and impact.

Investigation and Resolution

We will initiate a thorough investigation into the complaint, including gathering relevant information and consulting involved parties.

Our goal is to provide a fair and timely resolution. If necessary, we will work with external parties to gather additional information.

Communication and Updates

We will keep the complainant informed of the progress of the investigation and the expected timeline for resolution.

If there are delays or changes to the resolution process, we will provide updates promptly.

Escalation

If the complainant is not satisfied with the initial resolution, they can request an escalation to a senior member of our team.

We are committed to addressing escalated complaints in a timely manner.

Confidentiality and Privacy

All complaints and related information will be treated confidentially and in compliance with data protection laws.

Feedback and Improvement

Feedback from complaints is valuable to us and contributes to our continuous improvement.

We will analyze trends in complaints to identify opportunities for enhancing our services.
